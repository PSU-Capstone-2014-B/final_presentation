Background of ITS and Portal

ITS

  • Intelligent Transportation Systems
  • Collects and analyzes transportation information

PORTAL

  • Portland, Oregon Regional Transportation Archive Listing
  • Archives various traffic information in the Portland metro area

ITS is an organization that has a place here at PSU. It stands for "Intelligent Transportation Systems". ITS' role here is to collect and analyze various information about traffic. PORTAL is a sub-group within ITS that has a more specific role. It stands for Portland, Oregon Regional Transportation Archive Listing. Their goal is to collect and archive various traffic data and statistics in the Portland Metro area.
