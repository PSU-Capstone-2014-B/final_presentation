Basic Overview of Routelandia

    Android App Presents travel times on highways
  • near Portland displaced from origin historical data
    • Traffic is vaguely cyclical Lets you know best time to hit highway

Routelandia is an app that runs natively on android phones which shows travel times on highways. As our data is local to the general Portland area, those are the highways Routelandia will be able to display. The most exciting part is this app won't just give you travel times for the moment you choose, but also for before and after. It does this by looking at information from the near past at that time to project how it will be. Traffic tends to follow cycles, as anyone who deals with rush-hour each morning can tell you. Routelandia lets you see, in a convenient way, what those cycles look like, letting you know when is the best time to hit the road.

Interesting technical challenges: Backend

A REST Service

  • RESTler framework to abstract away the nitty gritty of REST
  • Communicates with frontend via JSON
  • Does the majority of the number crunching
  • Communicates with PORTALs database to generate statistics
  • BDD testing with Behat
  • All written in PHP using Model View Controller
